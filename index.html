Hi, I'm Sriram

I'm a dedicated Mechanical Engineer from Andhra Pradesh, India. I'm driven by curiosity and my passion to yearn for the beyond. I did my undergrad at IIT Bhubaneswar from 2016-20. I have been trying to find myself ever since. I have worked in different roles in different positions ever since. Throughout these years, I have gained a certain set of skills owing to my academic and professional research experience.

I have spent all of my professional career involved in various fields: A student in Mechanical Engineering, Summer intern in Machine Learning, Researcher in Thermal and CFD area, Engineer in Supply Chain Management, and Deputy Engineer in R&D for Thermal Management and structure design for ruggedized systems. I believe that my strength lies in my ability to integrate theory and practicality at every stage and being wired towards problem-solving. I have excelled in all fields of Mechanical Engineering, both academically and industrially during my work experience at Bharat Electronics Limited. However, my interests are more inclined towards Thermal Management and Computational Fluid Dynamics.

Beyond the world of engineering, I find joy in getting immersed in movies, cycling through scenic routes, trekking amidst nature, and experimenting with cooking, alongside many other seasonal hobbies. I have tried to flesh out my personal accomplishments in the Others section. These experiences remind us that creativity and new ideas can flourish beyond the limits we impose on ourselves. Let's work together to devise meaningful solutions through engineering.
